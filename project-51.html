Project 01

51WestFilms

A portfolio website for a Haarlem-based film company, designed to showcase their work and convert visitors into clients.

Role
Designer / Developer
Tools
HTML / CSS / WordPress
Year
2025
Links
Live project ↗ GitHub ↗

Overview

51WestFilms is a starting audiovisual company based in Haarlem. They needed a website that presents their work professionally while also making it easy for potential clients to get in touch. The goal was to create a clean and visually focused platform where their projects take center stage, while still supporting key actions like contacting the team and finding their social channels. The challenge was to balance strong visual presentation with clear structure — making sure the site feels high-quality without becoming overwhelming or difficult to navigate.

My role

This was a solo project where I was responsible for the entire process, from concept to execution.

  • Defined the concept and direction based on research into AI usage and transparency
  • Designed and prototyped the full experience from early ideas to interactive flows
  • Developed multiple iterations to refine usability and clarity
  • Built and tested digital interactions to communicate AI presence effectively
  • Translated abstract ideas into a concrete and understandable product

Process

I started by identifying the core goal of the website: showcasing audiovisual work while making it easy for potential clients to take action. From there, I explored references from similar film and portfolio websites to understand how visual content is best presented. Based on this, I defined a minimal structure where the work remains central, supported by clear navigation and accessible contact options. I moved into design by creating initial layouts and testing different ways to display video content without cluttering the interface. The focus was on hierarchy, spacing, and keeping the experience clean. After defining the direction, I translated the design into a working website. I developed multiple iterations, refining layout, responsiveness, and interaction details to improve usability across devices. Throughout the process, I continuously evaluated how the site feels in use, making sure users can quickly understand the brand, view the work, and find ways to get in touch or access social channels.

Result

The final result is a clean and professional portfolio website that gives 51WestFilms a strong online presence. The platform highlights their work while supporting key actions like contacting the team and navigating to their social media. It provides a solid foundation for a growing company to present themselves and attract new clients. Looking back, I would explore adding more motion or interaction to better reflect the dynamic nature of video work. While the current version is clear and effective, there’s room to push the experience further visually.
