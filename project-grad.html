Graduation Project - FaunAI

Overview

FaunAI is a concept project that explores how AI operates in the background of everyday digital experiences, often without users being aware of it. The goal was to make this invisible layer visible and understandable, especially for students who use AI tools daily but rarely question how they work. The project focuses on transparency and awareness, showing when and how AI is influencing content, decisions, or interactions. Instead of adding friction, the concept integrates subtle visual cues and interactions that inform users without disrupting their experience.

My role

This was a solo project where I was responsible for the entire process, from concept to execution.

  • Defined the concept and direction based on research into AI usage and transparency
  • Designed and prototyped the full experience from early ideas to interactive flows
  • Developed multiple iterations to refine usability and clarity
  • Built and tested digital interactions to communicate AI presence effectively
  • Translated abstract ideas into a concrete and understandable product

Process

I started with research into how students use AI tools and how aware they are of AI’s role in their daily interactions. By analyzing existing platforms and behaviors, I identified a gap: AI is widely used, but rarely visible or understood. Based on these insights, I defined the concept of making AI visible without disrupting the user experience. The challenge was to communicate something abstract in a way that feels natural and intuitive. From there, I moved into rapid iteration. I developed multiple concepts and prototypes, starting with rough explorations to test different ways of visualizing AI. These evolved into more refined and interactive designs, with each iteration focusing on improving clarity, usability, and subtlety. Testing and feedback played an important role in shaping the final direction. I continuously evaluated whether the concept was understandable and whether the visual cues added value without overwhelming the user.

Result

The final result is an interactive concept that demonstrates how AI transparency can be integrated into digital products in a subtle but effective way. It shows how users can become more aware of AI without needing technical knowledge, simply through better design and interaction. Looking back, I would push the concept further by testing it in more real-world scenarios. While the prototype communicates the idea clearly, integrating it into existing platforms would provide deeper insights into its impact.
